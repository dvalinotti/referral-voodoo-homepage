Word of Mouth Marketing

ReferralVoodoo is a cloud-based application that makes it simple and easy to activate / incent your existing customers to bring in new business. Unlike most referral or affiliate marketing software, ReferralVoodoo works with businesses that do not have an e-commerce transaction system.

How It Works

In its simplest form, ReferralVoodoo works like a standard referral card program. A card (or email / text) is sent from a happy customer to their friend that they want to refer. The friend is offered an incentive or reward if they become a customer of the business.

THE PROCESS

Step 1

Invite your best patients to recommend your practice and become your Ambassador.

Step 2

New ambassadors send customized messages to friends and family inviting them your practice.

Step 3

When new patients make an appointment, ReferralVoodoo attributes these patients to individual Ambassadors.

Step 4

Recognize the individual Ambassador contributions in the ReferralVoodoo dashboard.
